About Comfort Alarm

Comfort Alarm (위로 알람) is an alarm clock application that wakes you up with vibration and sound, and shows a personalized, AI-generated comfort/advice message tailored to how you're feeling that day.

Key features include:

  • Custom alarm time and repeat-day scheduling
  • Background alarms that ring with sound and vibration even when the app is closed
  • A full-screen comfort message shown when the alarm rings, generated based on what you've shared about that day of the week
  • A status-bar notification showing the current time and your next alarm
  • Snooze and dismiss controls
  • Multi-language support (Korean, English, Japanese)

The App does not require account registration or login credentials. An internet connection is only used to generate your personalized comfort message; alarm scheduling, ringing, and notifications all work fully offline.

Information We Collect

Comfort Alarm does not require you to create an account, and we do not operate any server that stores your personal information.

To personalize your comfort messages, you may optionally enter information such as:

  • Your name
  • What you're worried about or struggling with on specific days of the week
  • Your happiest and hardest days of the week
  • A person who causes you distress
  • Things you're worried about
  • A person or thing you miss
  • Words you'd like to say to yourself

All of this information is stored locally on your device only (in local app storage), and is never uploaded to any server operated by us. We do not collect device identifiers, precise location, contacts, or photos.

How Your Comfort Message Is Generated

When a comfort message needs to be generated, the information you've entered (your name and the details listed above) is sent, over the internet, directly to Google's Generative Language API (Gemini) as part of a text prompt, so it can write a personalized message for you. This is the only situation in which information you enter leaves your device.

This data is processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control how long Google retains this data. If you'd prefer not to share this information, you may leave the related fields blank — the app will still function and will show a default comfort message instead.

Once generated, your comfort message is cached locally on your device so that it can still be shown on the alarm screen even when your phone has no internet connection at the moment the alarm rings.

Permissions

The App uses the following permissions:

  • INTERNET – Sends your prompt to Google's Gemini API to generate your comfort message
  • VIBRATE – Vibrates the device when the alarm rings
  • WAKE_LOCK – Briefly wakes the device so the alarm can start reliably
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_EXACT_ALARM – Lets the alarm fire at the exact time you set, even in Doze/battery-saving mode
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE – Keeps the alarm-ringing service and the persistent clock notification running
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT – Displays the alarm and comfort message screen over the lock screen
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Shows the alarm notification and the status-bar clock/next-alarm notification
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – Re-arms your alarms and the clock notification after the device restarts
  • DISABLE_KEYGUARD – Allows the alarm screen to dismiss the lock screen when it rings

The App does not request access to:

  • Location services
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • SMS messages
  • Photos or media files
  • Phone state

Third-Party Services

Comfort Alarm uses Google's Generative Language API (Gemini) solely to generate your personalized comfort message, as described above.

The App does not display advertisements, and does not use any analytics, tracking, or behavioral profiling services.

Data Security

Your personal reflections and alarm settings are stored locally on your device. Because we do not operate a server that stores this information, the risk of it being exposed through us is minimized.

While we strive to keep the App secure, no software can guarantee absolute security under all circumstances, and we cannot control the security practices of third-party services such as Google.

Children's Privacy

Comfort Alarm is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any information entered into the App is stored locally on the user's own device.

International Users

Comfort Alarm is available worldwide.

Information you choose to enter for your comfort message may be transmitted internationally to Google's servers for processing, as described above. Aside from this, the App does not otherwise transfer personal information internationally.
